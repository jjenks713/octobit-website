About

Octobit is not new to the bass music scene, spending his younger days djing and spreading the music that influences him around Salt Lake City. He has spent the last few years taking a break from djing and crafting his unique sound as a bass music producer.

With support from; Krewella, Alison Wonderland, INHUMAN (formerly Code: Pandorum), Vampa, Perses, Mike’s Revenge, OMAS, Futuretwist and Heaven Fell, releases on Crowsnest Audio, Play Me Records, Simplify., Rude Service, MMXVAC, Recall Records, Emengy, MDP Records, Basscvlt, Deathstar Cult and more. Octobit, with his experimental sounds and attention to detail in his production will get you dancing and shake the venue.

Octobits love for all genres shows in his production and he will be turning heads all around the world with his original music! Dubstep, Trap, Drum n Bass, Midtempo, Electro/basshouse. A jack of all trades, look out because Octobit will be bringing the bass!

Octobit is a heavy bass music producer with melodic origins. Check out the videos, downloads and follow on socials for so many more things to come
